Current core track

Public Benchmark Track

This is the current operating core of Predictomat: short-duration Polymarket events used as public anchors for comparing crowd probability, structured forecast estimates, and how divergences evolve over time.

What this is today

More than a forecast log

The goal is not just to publish a number. Each benchmark makes the comparison visible: market probability, structured forecast, current divergence, peak divergence, and whether later price action moved toward the earlier view.

Where this is going

A broader forecasting platform

The benchmark layer is the starting point, not the final product. Predictomat is meant to expand toward trigger-based revisions, explicit update logs, calibration and scorekeeping, and later independent analyses beyond market-anchored comparisons.

Prepared second track

Greenfield Analyses

This section is being prepared for independent short-horizon forecast analyses that do not begin with a public market quote. It is the natural next layer of Predictomat beyond benchmark-only publishing.

What will appear here

Independent event theses, scenario framing, and structured forecasts where the analysis is driven by the real-world question itself rather than by an existing market price.

What will stay consistent

Short time horizons, explicit triggers for revision, transparent documentation of changes, and later review of calibration and forecast quality.

Format still being defined

This area may later evolve into a thesis feed, journal-style stream, or card-based analysis layer. For now, it is a visible and flexible structural starting point.
